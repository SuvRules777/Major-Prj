Validation Methodology: Ground-truth weights are sourced from fish_measurements.csv containing verified lab measurements for 7 species. Predicted weights are computed using the standard allometric length–weight equation W = a × Lb, where species-specific coefficients a and b were fitted via least-squares regression on the training split. Accuracy is defined as 100% − |Percentage Error| per sample. The overall model accuracy (100% − MAPE) quantifies the average predictive performance across all validation samples.
